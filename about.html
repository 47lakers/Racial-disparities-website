IRA PORCHIA

Hi, thanks for visiting this my website.

My name is Ira Porchia and as a young black man, I created this website to shed light on the trials that black men and women have to go through on a daily basis. Not all black men and women are represented by these statistics on this website, but as you will see, a majority of them are represented by these statistics. My goal in creating this website is to help facilitate change by causing people of all races and ethnicities to realize what their black brothers and sisters are dealing with.
